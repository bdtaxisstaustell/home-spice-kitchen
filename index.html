Our Story — From Heart to Home

For Ms Trina, cooking has never been just about food — it has always been about care, comfort, and connection.

From a young age, she found joy in the kitchen. The warmth of a home-cooked meal and the smiles that followed became her passion.

For eight years, she carried a dream — to share real homemade Indian and Bangladeshi food beyond her home.

Today, that dream has come to life.

This is not restaurant food.
This is food made with care, tradition, and heart — just like home.
