Our Story — From Heart to Home

For Ms Trina, cooking has never been just about food — it has always been about care, comfort, and connection.

From a young age, she found joy in the kitchen. The sound of spices crackling, the warmth of a home-cooked meal, and the smiles that followed — this is where her passion truly lived. Over the years, she became known among family and friends not just as someone who could cook, but as someone who cooked with heart. Every dish carried a sense of home.

For the past eight years, Ms Trina held onto a quiet dream — to share her food with more people, not as restaurant-style dishes, but as proper homemade Indian and Bangladeshi meals, just the way they are meant to be. Honest. Traditional. Full of soul.

Life moved on, responsibilities came and went, but that dream never faded.

Today, that dream has finally come to life.

Home Spice Catering is not just a kitchen — it is the result of years of passion, patience, and belief. Every meal prepared here reflects Ms Trina’s journey, her love for cooking, and her commitment to keeping food real and homemade.

This is not restaurant food.

This is food made with care, with tradition, and with heart — just like home.
